About me

I obtained my B.S. degree at the School of Automation, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), Nanjing, China, in 2019. I am currently pursuing the Ph. D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from the the School of Automation under the supervision of Prof. Haoping Wang, NJUST, Nanjing, China.

Research Interest

  • Switched Systems (Linear/Affine/Nonlinear/Markov) and Hybrid Systems.

  • Time-Delay Systems.

  • Sampled-Data Observability and Observer Design.
